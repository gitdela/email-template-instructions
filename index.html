Buy Cryptocurrency - No Account Needed

1. Select option 1: Buy

2. Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to purchase. 1: Bitcoin or 2: USDT (Tether)

3. Enter the amount you want to purchase in USD

4. Review the purchase amount and provide a valid wallet address to receive the cryptocurrency

5. You will receive a payment prompt. Enter your PIN to authorize and complete the transaction securely.

Sell Cryptocurrency

1. Select option 2: Sell

2. Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to sell. 1: Bitcoin or 2: USDT (Tether)

3. Enter the amount you want to sell in USD

5. Review the amount and provide a valid wallet address to receive the cryptocurrency

6. Wait for the pop up and confirm transaction.

Send Cryptocurrency

Note: This option is exclusively available to Mybitstore account holders

1. Select option 3: Send

2. Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to send. 1: Bitcoin or 2: USDT (Tether)

3. Review your Mybitstore account balance and provide the amount you want to send in USD

4. Enter a valid wallet address to send the cryptocurrency to.

Receive Cryptocurrency

1. Select option 4: Receive

2. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to receive 1: Bitcoin 2: USDT (Tether)

3. Your wallet address will be sent to you SMS or email.

4. Copy the provided wallet address and share it with your sender to receive the funds securely into your Mybitstore account.
